# Privacy Statement

Sticky Notes for Business Central stores sticky note data inside the target Business Central environment.

## Data Stored

The extension stores the following data in Business Central:

- note message content
- note colour and display style
- schedule and expiry dates
- target record reference information
- created by
- created at
- active status

## Personal Data

The `Created By` field may contain a user identifier from Business Central. The extension does not intentionally collect personal data beyond what is needed to identify who created a note.

## Data Processing

- data is stored in the customer's Business Central environment
- the extension does not transmit note data to external services
- the extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services

## Data Retention

Data remains in Business Central until it is deleted by an authorized user or administrator.
